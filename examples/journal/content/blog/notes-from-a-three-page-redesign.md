# Notes From A Three-Page Redesign

Most redesigns become expensive because they quietly turn into system rewrites.

## The tighter version

Choose three pages that matter, make them legible, and stop there.

1. Homepage
2. Article page
3. Archive or listing page

Everything else usually becomes easier once those three pages have a clear voice.
