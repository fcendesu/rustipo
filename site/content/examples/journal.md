# Journal Example

The journal example is a writing-first Rustipo site. It is the best example when the reading experience matters as much as the content model.

## Best Fit

Choose this example when you want:

- an essay or blog-focused site
- a publication feel instead of a generic personal page
- archive pages and post flow to feel central
- a stronger editorial tone out of the box

## What It Uses

- example directory: `examples/journal/`
- theme: `journal`
- palette: `catppuccin-frappe`

## What It Shows

- built-in `journal` theme
- menu configuration
- blog archive output
- project pages alongside long-form writing
- typography choices that support longer reading sessions

## Why It Matters

This example shows how Rustipo can feel more publication-like without turning into a larger framework or requiring a complex app shell.

## Good Next Step

Use this example as your reference when the site should feel writing-led.
